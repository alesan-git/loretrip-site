## HOW IT WORKS SECTION (optional, for more detail)

### Headline
How LoreTrip works

**1. Client sends a request**
"We want 10 days, nature, food, something authentic, maybe Slovenia or Croatia, not too much driving."

**2. LoreTrip structures the trip**
Traveler profile, trip rhythm, destination fit, route logic, experience ideas — organized and editable.

**3. You refine and approve**
Adjust the itinerary, add insider notes, lock what works, remove what doesn't. Your expertise shapes the final result.

**4. Client receives a branded proposal**
A polished, professional itinerary that looks like it came from your best advisor on their best day — in a fraction of the time.
