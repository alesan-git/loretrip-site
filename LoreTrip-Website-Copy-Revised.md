## THE REAL GAP IN AI TRAVEL [NEW — insert after The Problem]

### AI recommends what's popular. Agencies recommend what's good.

Generic AI trip planners pull from the same pool: aggregated reviews, sponsored listings, SEO-optimized content. The result? Every destination starts to look the same. The same 10 restaurants. The same "hidden gems" that 4 million people visited last year.

The agencies who actually live and breathe their destinations know better. They know the restaurant where the food is genuinely great — not the one with 4.8 stars because tourists don't know what else to compare it to. They know the bike rental that actually maintains their fleet. The viewpoint that isn't on Instagram yet.

That knowledge has always been locked in the advisor's head, delivered one email at a time.

LoreTrip unlocks it. Agencies curate their trusted partners, places, and experiences in a dashboard. The AI prioritizes those curated picks in every itinerary it generates — confidently, contextually, and on-brand. The result is a trip plan that reflects what the agency genuinely believes is the best way to experience the destination.

Not the most reviewed. Not the most affiliated. The most trusted.

## WORKFLOW — FROM CLIENT BRIEF TO BRANDED PROPOSAL [REPLACE]

### Seven steps. One workflow.

LoreTrip supports the workflow from curation to client handoff — without taking the advisor out of the loop.

**00 — Curate**
Before any traveler asks a question, your agency builds its knowledge layer. Mark your trusted partners — the family-run hotel by the lake, the wine bar your guide swears by, the kayak outfitter that's never late. Set priority levels. Add notes. Flag seasonal relevance. This curated layer becomes the AI's first preference. When a traveler asks for "a great lunch near the coast," the AI doesn't search the internet. It reaches for what you've already approved.

**01 — Understand**
Capture traveler preferences, constraints, and intent from natural conversation.

**02 — Structure**
Convert loose requests into a clear trip plan you can edit and refine.

**03 — Draft**
Generate coherent itineraries with day-by-day plans and destination narrative. Every AI suggestion draws from your curated pool first. When you review a draft, the recommendations already reflect your standards — not generic data. You're refining from a strong base, not correcting from scratch.

**04 — Refine**
Adjust, lock, remove, and reshape anything before the client sees it.

**05 — Present**
Deliver a polished, branded proposal that reflects your agency — not a generic AI tool.

**06 — Monetize**
Service fees, accommodation margin, activities, and supplier partnerships — built in.

## HOW IT WORKS — VISUAL WALKTHROUGH [REPLACE]

### A real proposal, in minutes — not days.

**01 — Client request**
Start from how clients actually talk.

A client sends a brief: "We want 10 days, nature, food, something authentic, maybe the coast and the mountains, not too much driving." LoreTrip turns that into a structured trip profile your advisors can shape.

```
// CLIENT BRIEF
10 days · two travelers · slow pace
Nature, regional food, low driving load
Region: coast + highlands
Budget tier: mid-to-upper, advisor-set
```

**02 — Advisor refinement**
The AI drafts. You decide.

Every recommendation is editable, removable, and advisor-approved before it reaches the client. Lock the parts that work. Replace the parts that don't. Insert the supplier you actually trust.

Locked: Day 3 · river valley walk + traditional lunch
Replaced: Generic hotel → partner guesthouse
Note: Ferry connection, Mon only

## WHAT THE TRAVELER GETS [NEW — insert after How It Works]

### A trip that feels personally recommended, not algorithmically generated.

Travelers can browse ready-made packages or build from scratch. They can take a 12-day itinerary and say "make it 9 days" or "swap the lake day for a wine region." The AI adjusts — but every swap still draws from the agency's curated knowledge. The human touch doesn't disappear when the traveler customizes.

Many travelers love a package but wish they could tweak it — an extra day here, a different activity there. With LoreTrip, that flexibility is built in. The 80%-right package becomes 100%-right, and every modification still respects the agency's curated quality.

The difference is subtle but powerful: the traveler feels like they're getting advice from someone who knows the country. Because they are. The AI is the delivery mechanism. The agency is the intelligence.
